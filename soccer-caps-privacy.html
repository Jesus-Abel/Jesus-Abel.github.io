Privacy Policy


This is the Privacy Policy of Soccer Caps.

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Soccer Caps for Android mobile devices created by Abel Beltrán.

What information do we collect?

We collect and access only the information provided by Google through Google AdMob and Google Play Console services. This includes, but is not limited to, device information, app usage data, and user demographics. We do not collect any personal data directly from users. All information is handled in accordance with Google's privacy policies and is used solely to improve our services and provide a better user experience.

We use Google-supplied app performance tracking information to enhance the application.

This application uses your age to determine what types of ads we can show you. We do not collect data from children to display ads tailored to their preferences.

This application uses Google AdMob services to show ads to users. All ads displayed in the application have been approved by Google.

Google AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl
