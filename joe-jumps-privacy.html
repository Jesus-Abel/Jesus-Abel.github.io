Privacy Policy


This is the Privacy Policy of Joe Jumps.

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Joe Jumps for Android mobile devices created by Abel Beltrán.

What information do we collect?

We collect and access only the information provided by Google through Google Play Console services. This includes, but is not limited to, device information, app usage data, and user demographics. We do not collect any personal data directly from users. All information is handled in accordance with Google's privacy policies and is used solely to improve our services and provide a better user experience.

We use Google-supplied app performance tracking information to enhance the application.

Google Analytics

This application uses Google Analytics to help us analyze how you use the app. This information helps us improve the product. Through Google Analytics, we know how long you use the app, what kind of device you're using, your location, and more. All this data is used to enhance the app's functionality and user experience.
